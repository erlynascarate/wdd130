Peru

Peru, officially the Republic of Peru, is a sovereign country located in western South America. It borders Ecuador and Colombia to the north; Brazil and Bolivia to the east; Chile to the south; and the Pacific Ocean to the west, which borders its 2,250 kilometers of coastline. Its territory is composed of diverse landscapes: valleys, plateaus, and high peaks. The Andes stretch westward toward the desert coast and eastward toward the Amazon. It is one of the countries with the most biological diversity and mineral resources in the world.
