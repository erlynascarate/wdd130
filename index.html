Peru

Peru is a country in South America encompassing a section of the Amazon rainforest and Machu Picchu, an ancient Inca city high in the Andes. The region around Machu Picchu, including the Sacred Valley, the Inca Trail, and the colonial city of Cusco, is rich in archaeological sites. On Peru's arid Pacific coast lies Lima, the capital, with a preserved colonial center and important collections of pre-Columbian art.
